About Us

Huda Real Estate, established in 2009 E.C., has emerged as a leading force in Ethiopia's real estate industry. Under the visionary leadership of Omar Yasin, Huda has been instrumental in shaping urban landscapes... and setting new standards for quality, innovation, and sustainability. Specializing in residential, commercial, and industrial projects, Huda has successfully delivered numerous landmark developments. These projects have not only transformed the skyline but have also earned accolades for their architectural brilliance and commitment to sustainable practices. Huda's unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction, coupled with a robust financial foundation, has solidified its position as a trusted and forward-thinking real estate enterprise. The company's ability to consistently deliver high-quality projects on time and within budget has earned the loyalty of clients and investors alike.
